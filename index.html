What is MeeCards and how is it different from a visiting card?
+
MeeCards is a living digital identity profile — not a static card. A paper card gets lost, can't be updated, and gives you zero data. MeeCards is a link and QR code that opens your full professional profile: photo, contact, portfolio, documents, and a WhatsApp connect button. It updates in real-time and tracks who viewed it.
Does the person receiving my card need an app?
+
No. Your MeeCard profile opens in any browser — Chrome, Safari, anything. The person receiving your card doesn't need to download or install anything. Zero friction.
How does the NFC card work?
+
An NFC card has a tiny chip embedded inside. When you tap it against the back of a modern smartphone (Android or iPhone 7+), the phone reads the chip and automatically opens your MeeCards profile. No scanning, no typing. Just tap and connect.
Can I update my profile after printing?
+
Yes — that's MeeCards' biggest advantage. Your QR code and NFC card always point to the same profile URL. You can change your number, company, photo, or any detail anytime. The physical card never needs to be replaced.
Is my personal data and documents safe?
+
Absolutely. Your documents are stored with bank-level encryption. Only you control who can see them. We never sell your data to third parties.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept all major Indian payment methods — UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit & credit cards, net banking, and EMI. All payments are processed securely via Razorpay.
Is there a free plan? What does it include?
+
Yes, MeeCards is free forever for basic use. The free plan includes your profile link, QR code, WhatsApp connect button, and 1 document upload. Upgrade to Pro (₹99/month) for unlimited documents, lead capture, analytics, and a custom URL.
