What is TapID and how is it different from a visiting card?
+
TapID is a living digital identity profile — not a static card. A paper card gets lost, can't be updated, and gives you zero data. TapID is a link and QR code that opens your full professional profile: photo, contact, portfolio, documents, and a WhatsApp connect button. It updates in real-time and tracks who viewed it.
Does the person receiving my card need an app?
+
No. Your TapID profile opens in any browser — Chrome, Safari, anything. The person receiving your card doesn't need to download or install anything. Zero friction.
How does the NFC card work?
+
An NFC card has a tiny chip embedded inside. When you tap it against the back of a modern smartphone (Android or iPhone 7+), the phone reads the chip and automatically opens your TapID profile. No scanning, no typing. Just tap and connect.
Can I update my profile after downloading or printing?
+
Yes — that's one of TapID's biggest advantages. Your QR code and NFC card always point to the same profile URL. You can change your phone number, company, photo, or any detail at any time. The physical card or printed QR code never needs to be replaced.
Is my personal data and documents safe?
+
Absolutely. Your documents are stored with bank-level encryption. Only you control who can see them. We never sell your data to third parties.
How long does NFC card delivery take?
+
NFC cards are shipped across India via courier and arrive within 5–7 business days. Your digital card is ready instantly — the physical card just arrives later.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept all major Indian payment methods — UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit & credit cards, net banking, and EMI. All payments are processed securely via Razorpay.
Is there a free plan? What does it include?
+
Yes, TapID is free forever for basic use. The free plan includes your profile link, QR code, WhatsApp connect button, and 1 document upload. Upgrade to Pro (₹99/month) for unlimited documents, lead capture, analytics, and a custom profile URL.
